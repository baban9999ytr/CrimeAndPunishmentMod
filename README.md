Crime and Punishment: A Soulbound Moral System

Crime and Punishment is an atmospheric gameplay overhaul that introduces a deep, mechanical "Guilt" system to Minecraft. This mod forces players to face the psychological and social consequences of senseless violence, turning every strike against the innocent into a heavy burden that affects combat, trade, and survival.

### The Sin System

Senseless violence no longer goes unnoticed by the world. Taking the life of a Villager, a tamed pet, or a passive baby animal triggers an immediate state of Guilt.

The Haunting: Upon committing a crime, the player is struck with temporary Darkness and Nausea while a chilling monologue appears in the action bar.

The Timer: Guilt lasts for 72,000 ticks (one full hour of real-time play) and persists through death.

Acoustic Shift: When a crime is committed, global music is silenced to emphasize the gravity of the player's actions.

### Chains of the Honorless

The physical manifestation of your sin, the Chains of the Honorless, are automatically added to your inventory upon committing a crime.

Soulbound Burden: These EPIC-rarity chains are fire-resistant and cannot be easily discarded.

Combat Debuffs: While held or equipped, the chains significantly reduce your Attack Damage, Attack Speed, and Max Health.

Anti-Utility: The weight of the chains makes it nearly impossible to mine or harvest blocks effectively.

### The Social Exile

The world reacts to your "Honorless" status in real-time, making village life dangerous and expensive.

The Iron Judge: Naturally spawned Iron Golems can "sense" the chains. Unless you have the Bad Omen effect, they will attack you on sight.

The Guilt Tax: Villagers recognize your burden and will significantly inflate their prices. Expect to pay a heavy premium (at least 5 extra emeralds per trade) for all goods.

Mercy in War: If you approach a Golem while carrying the chains but also possessing a Bad Omen, the Golem will stand down, acknowledging that you are there to protect the village from a raid.

### The Path to Redemption

There are only three ways to lift the weight of the chains:

Patience: Carry the burden for the full 72,000 ticks until the guilt naturally fades.

The Hero’s Path: Win a Raid and obtain the Hero of the Village effect. This immediately cleanses your soul and removes the chains.

The Cleric’s Indulgence: Visit a Village Cleric. These spiritual leaders offer a "cleansing" trade—exchanging 30 Gold Ingots to reduce your guilt by 2,000 ticks per transaction.

Once redeemed, the chains vanish, your experience is boosted, and you unlock a unique Challenge Advancement to mark your return to honor.
